Why to use strong passwords or should i say pass-phrases?

Hey ppl,
Now-a-days hacking has become part of internet culture
While there are some who use this skill for good,
there is other side to the coin too! Some try to steal
private data for their gain and your pain, and main reason
the're able to break into your data/info. is due to weak passwords.
It is very easy to crack a password if it has very less variation
in it's characters.

Hackers mostly use following techniques
to crack your password:

  • brute force
  • dictionary attack
  • rainbow table attack

This way your password just pops open like a soda can. If it is weak.

Only Way to avoid is use a strong password which should be mixture of
Lower-case, upper-case alphabets; Numbers; Special characters(punctuation).
And make sure that site you share your data with should use secure channel to
communicate with your computer like (ssl, ssh, https, etc).
